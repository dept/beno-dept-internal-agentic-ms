# Vision: Agentic Managed Services

## Agentic Managed Services

Agentic Managed Services combines human delivery teams with governed AI agents that can understand project context, propose changes, and accelerate support workflows.

The objective is practical: reduce time-to-context, improve change safety, and make operational knowledge durable.

## Agentic Project Delivery

Project delivery evolves from ad hoc documentation to structured, AI-consumable context. Teams maintain a versioned `.ai` folder as part of delivery quality, enabling:
- faster onboarding;
- predictable implementation support;
- stronger incident response continuity.

## Future AI Agents

Target agent families:
- Discovery agents (project mapping and context generation)
- Change-planning agents (impact/risk analysis)
- Incident agents (triage and runbook assistance)
- QA/release agents (test gap and release readiness checks)
- Governance agents (standards and policy enforcement)

## Commercial Opportunities

A standardized agentic delivery model enables DEPT to offer:
- accelerated project transitions into Managed Services;
- premium operational intelligence packages;
- differentiated support SLAs with AI-assisted response capabilities;
- reusable cross-client delivery accelerators.

## Benefits for DEPT and Clients

### DEPT
- Lower onboarding overhead per project
- Repeatable managed services playbooks
- Better knowledge retention across squads

### Clients
- Faster, safer change cycles
- Improved transparency in operations and dependencies
- Higher resilience through documented runbooks and context
